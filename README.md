# LeadFlow

Find local clients without spending hours searching.

---

## What is this?

LeadFlow is a simple tool that helps you find local businesses you can contact.

Instead of manually searching Google or maps for hours, you enter:
- what you do
- what kind of businesses you want
- where

And you get a list of real potential clients.

---

## Why I built this

Finding clients is one of the hardest parts when you're starting or working freelance.

Most people:
- don’t know where to look
- spend too much time searching
- give up too early

I wanted something simple that removes that friction.

---

## What it does (for now)

- Finds local businesses based on a type + location  
- Returns real businesses you can contact  
- Saves hours of manual research  

---

## Example

Input:
- Target: Restaurants  
- Location: Paris  

Output:
- List of restaurants  
- Address  
- Website  
- Basic contact info
